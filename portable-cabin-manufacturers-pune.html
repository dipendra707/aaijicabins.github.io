Portable Cabin

Keeping in mind the diverse requirements of our clients, we are involved in offering a wide range of Portable Cabins. Offered products are projected in a manner to offer reduced maintenance cost and spacious interior solutions. Our experts hold out the designing as per the prevailing market demands and thus, assure our offered range to be of customers’ expectations. Used prominently in offices, factory sheds and other commercial places, these cabins have gained immense admiration.

These cabins are manufactured using high-grade raw material, which is sourced from the reliable vendors of the market. Our organization also offers these portable cabins on hiring basis to corporate houses & offices.

Our expertise lies in meeting the fabrication demands of mild steel based Portable Cabins. These are precision fabricated at our facilities using quality tested steel and are duly welded to provide better stability. It comprises of entry doors & windows, self-draining roof, base frame, peripheral structure as per the requirements. Further, eight corners of these Cabins are sufficiently strengthened to facilitate transportation, lifting and placing operations.
