We offer a wide range of portable cabins that are the best answer to urgent accommodation requirements almost anywhere. They are designed to meet the demands such as those associated with rapid development and are especially suitable for use in adverse climatic conditions. Flexibility in its construction and assembly makes it possible to meet an almost unlimited range of applications. The unit can be fabricated and assembled quickly in accordance with the client's specific requirements. Delivered in a single-lift floor chassis, mounted either on steel skids or support legs, the units are fully fitted and wired, ready for immediate use. Portable cabins are lift–n-shift cabins, designed for comfortable living. They are made by selection of sturdy design and durable materials, which can withstand extreme weather conditions and can be dismantled and re-assembled at any other location.
